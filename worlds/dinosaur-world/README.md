## **📖Introduction**

Welcome to _Magic Worlds_ where everything is possible and the only limit is your imagination and dreams. Forever free, family friendly and open source.

_Dinosaur World_ takes you on a journey back in time to the age of dinosaurs! Explore vast jungles, witness towering T-Rexes, and interact with AI-powered prehistoric creatures. Learn about the ancient ecosystem, uncover fossils, and test your survival skills in an immersive world filled with mystery and discovery.

## **🚀How to Access Dinosaur World**

1. **Launch Magic Worlds** and enter your spaceship.
2. **Talk to the Vice Captain** by walking up ahead and pressing `E`.
3. **Head straight up to the bridge** and enter by pressing `E`.
4. **Once in the bridge, climb up the platform** to access the travel console.
5. **Access the console** by entering the passcode `[789]`.
6. **Select Dinosaur World** in the World Hub.
7. **Start Exploring**: 7. **Start Exploring**: Encounter dinosaurs, dig up fossils, and navigate prehistoric landscapes.

## **🎮Gameplay & Features**

- 🦕 **Dinosaur Encounters** – Interact with AI-powered dinosaurs like the mighty T-Rex and Triceratops.
- ⛏️ **Fossil Hunting** – Dig up and assemble fossils to uncover the secrets of the past.
- 🌋 **Survival Mode** – Test your skills against the elements and ancient predators.
- 🌿 **Prehistoric Ecosystem** – Learn about dinosaurs and their habitats with real-time AI insights.
- 🎁 **Unlockable Rewards** – Earn exclusive prehistoric artifacts and collectibles.

### **🚨System Requirements**

#### Minimum:

- **OS**: Windows 10 (64-bit) / Linux / macOS 10.15+
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300
- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GTX 760 / AMD Radeon R7 260X
- **DirectX**: Version 11
- **Storage**: 50 GB available space
